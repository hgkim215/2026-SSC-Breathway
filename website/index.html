How It Works

One mission, four clear steps.

Breathway keeps the daily experience simple: check readiness, follow the breathing rhythm, move gently, then review the result.

Step 1

Check

Breathlessness, fatigue, confidence, and recent rhythm guide the mission level.

Step 2

Breathe

Guided inhale and exhale pacing helps create a stable starting rhythm.

Step 3

Move

Gentle sit-to-stand prompts keep movement approachable and easy to repeat.

Step 4

Result

Daily completion, streaks, and light progress feedback reinforce the habit.

Safety

Designed to feel reassuring, not demanding.

Breathway prioritizes calm pacing, simple exits, and fallback behavior. The product is intended as guided wellness support for routine building, not medical diagnosis or treatment.

Safety-first flow

Recommendation logic can reduce intensity when recent strain or repeated stops are detected.

Offline-first core

The core mission loop and local progress review remain available without backend dependency.

Gentle fallback behavior

If live microphone-based guidance is unavailable, the routine continues in rhythm-only mode.
